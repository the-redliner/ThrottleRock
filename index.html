Welcome to Throttle Rock

Nestled in the breathtaking hills of Kodaikanal, Tamil Nadu, Throttle Rock offers an unparalleled off-roading adventure. We are passionate riders dedicated to providing a full forest experience, combining adrenaline-pumping Enduro tracks with the tranquility of nature, comfortable stays, and delicious local cuisine.

Whether you're a seasoned rider or looking to try something new, our trails cater to various skill levels, all under the guidance of experienced professionals ensuring safety and fun.

What We Offer

Thrilling Enduro Tracks

Navigate challenging terrains, dense forest paths, and scenic routes designed for the ultimate Enduro experience. Our tracks offer a mix of technical sections and flowing trails.

Full Forest Immersion

Go beyond the ride. Immerse yourself in the pristine beauty of Kodaikanal's forests. Spot local flora and fauna, breathe in the fresh mountain air, and connect with nature.

Guided & Safe Adventures

Your safety is our priority. All rides are guided by experienced off-roaders. We provide essential safety briefings and ensure all equipment is top-notch.
